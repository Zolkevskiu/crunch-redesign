Viktoriia Zolotova in Sharjah Research Technology
and Innovation Park.

Viktoriia Zolotova

Viktoriia Zolotova , founder of Innovation Camp, a business tour around the world, recently visited Sharjah Research Technology and Innovation Park (SRTI Park). Aimed at managing the innovation ecosystem in the free zone, SRTI Park is an important center of entrepreneurship and innovation in the UAE.

Victoria took part in presentations and workshops to better understand how this platform can help to develop her own company and to achieve the goals of her clients – startups from different industries from all over the world.On December 3, she participated in an online presentation of Model World.

Sharjah Research Technology and Innovation Park

Established in 2016 by royal decree of H.H. Sheikh Sultan Bin Muhammad Al Qasimi, Ruler of Sharjah, United Arab Emirates, The Sharjah Research, Technology, and Innovation Park Free Zone (SRTIP) aims to develop and manage an innovation ecosystem that promotes Research and Development and supports enterprise activities and the triple helix collaboration of industry, government and academia.
